Interactive Elements: Duration (Time).

Brainstorming process.

My assigned interaction is Duration (Time).
I have been struggling to find existing projects that use Duration as the primary interaction and its history.
After thinking for ideas for a while, I decided to create a quiz where people have to guess what country first before doing the quiz. The hints of the country name will be shown after a particular time. The longer users stay on the site, the more suggestions they will get. The longer time they spend on the website waiting for the hint will increase the chance they will guess the country's name correctly. Eventually, they will be more likely to get higher scores on the quiz.
